Geopaparazzi VERSION


Geopaparazzi is brought to you by HydroloGIS.

For information contact the users mailinglist.

Geopaparazzi is an application for digital field mapping.

User documentation can be found on the homepage.

To import to and export GIS data from Geopaparazzi, look at this workshop.
The mapping engine is based on mapsforge.

The maps are from OpenStreetMap. OpenStreetMap is open data, licensed under the Open Data Commons Open Database License (ODbL).
© OpenStreetMap contributors

The charting engine is achartengine.

Spatialite support comes from the Spatialite-Android project.

Advanced geometries operations are performed by JTS.
